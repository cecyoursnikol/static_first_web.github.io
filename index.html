The Future of technology is Artificial Intelligence

Computer


Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the development of computer systems or software that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Mimic


AI aims to create machines that can mimic certain aspects of human cognitive functions and adapt to new situations, improving their performance over time.

Goal


The ultimate goal of AI is to develop systems that can exhibit general intelligence, allowing them to understand diverse tasks and contexts without being explicitly programmed for each specific instance.
